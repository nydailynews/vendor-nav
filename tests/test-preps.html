About Denver Post's Prep Stats

Welcome!

Welcome to The Denver Post's Post Preps section. Our goal is to make Post Preps the first site Colorado high school sports fans go to for stories, photos, video and statistics from all high school sports.

What's here and what's new

Use the navigation at the top of the page to find information on:

  • Prep teams, by schools,
  • Colorado prep sports by conference,
  • Colorado prep sports news,
  • Neil Devlin's All Things Colorado Preps blog,
  • Our Colorado prep photos,
  • and more.

We've added a lot to our prep sports offerings. Here is some of that:

  • Our new search by player name, school name and team name functionality
  • Indexes of schools organized by name and by the city of the school
  • Improved sport conference pages, with recent results, upcoming games as well as standings
  • Index of sports organized by season
  • Specific prep sport pages with results, schedule, and participating conferences and schools
  • Redesigned prep site navigation
